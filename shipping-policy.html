    Delivery coverage

    Kidvana ships across most serviceable pin codes in India. Delivery availability may depend on partner coverage, product category, and seasonal constraints.

    Standard delivery

    • Metro cities: 2 to 4 business days.
    • Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities: 3 to 6 business days.
    • Remote locations: 5 to 8 business days.

    Shipping fee

    • Free shipping above the minimum order threshold shown at checkout.
    • A small shipping fee may apply on low-value orders.
    • Special handling charges may apply for bulky items.

    Dispatch and delays

    • Orders are usually dispatched within 24 to 48 hours after confirmation.
    • Prepaid orders are prioritized for faster dispatch.
    • Festive seasons, weather events, or logistics disruptions may delay deliveries.
    • If an item becomes unavailable after order placement, our support team will contact you with alternatives or refund options.
