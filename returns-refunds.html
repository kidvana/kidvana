    Returns and Refunds

    We aim to keep returns simple and transparent for eligible orders.

    Policies

    Shipping Policy Returns and Refunds Privacy Policy Terms of Service Track Order

    Eligibility

    • Return requests should generally be raised within 7 days of delivery.
    • Products must be unused, unwashed, and returned with original packaging and tags.
    • Personal care, intimate wear, and certain clearance items may be non-returnable.

    Refund timelines

    • Prepaid orders: refund processed after quality check and sent back to original payment method.
    • Cash on Delivery orders: refund processed to bank account or store credit, depending on the chosen option.
    • Most refunds are completed within 5 to 7 business days after approval.

    How to request a return

    • Go to Track Order or contact support from the order details page.
    • Share your order ID, phone number, and issue details.
    • Our team will verify eligibility and arrange pickup when available.
