    How can I track my order?

    Use the Track Order page with your order ID and phone number.

    Which payment methods are supported?

    Kidvana supports Cash on Delivery and online payment through Razorpay when configured.

    Can I return an item?

    Eligible orders can usually be returned within the return window if the item is unused and in original packaging.
